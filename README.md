# Career Copilot AI

## Abstract

Career Copilot AI is an intelligent career guidance platform designed to assist students and job seekers in making informed career decisions. The system analyzes user resumes, identifies skill gaps, recommends relevant jobs, internships, and courses, and provides interview preparation support. By leveraging Natural Language Processing (NLP), Machine Learning, and Large Language Models (LLMs), the platform delivers personalized career roadmaps and opportunity recommendations, helping users improve employability and achieve their professional goals.

---

## Problem Statement

Many students and job seekers struggle to identify suitable career paths, understand industry requirements, and find relevant opportunities. Traditional career counseling is often expensive, time-consuming, and not personalized. There is a need for an AI-driven solution that can automatically analyze resumes, detect missing skills, recommend learning resources, match users with jobs, and provide career guidance in a scalable and accessible manner.

---

## Methodology

1. **Resume Upload & Parsing**

   * Users upload resumes in PDF or DOCX format.
   * NLP techniques extract skills, education, experience, and certifications.

2. **Skill Gap Analysis**

   * User skills are compared with desired job role requirements.
   * Missing skills are identified.

3. **Job Matching**

   * AI recommends jobs and internships based on profile compatibility.

4. **Course Recommendation**

   * Personalized learning resources and courses are suggested to bridge skill gaps.

5. **Interview Preparation**

   * AI generates role-specific interview questions and feedback.

6. **Opportunity Radar**

   * Recommends hackathons, scholarships, competitions, and internships.

7. **Dashboard & Reporting**

   * Displays ATS score, career progress, learning roadmap, and recommendations.

## Literature Survey

### 1. Resume Analysis Systems

Existing resume screening systems use NLP and machine learning to automate candidate evaluation. These systems improve recruitment efficiency but often lack personalized career guidance.

### 2. Recommendation Systems

Research on recommendation engines demonstrates the effectiveness of collaborative and content-based filtering in suggesting jobs and learning resources tailored to users.

### 3. AI Career Guidance Platforms

Modern AI-powered career counseling solutions provide job matching and skill assessments. However, many focus on a single aspect and do not offer an integrated career development ecosystem.

### 4. Large Language Models in Education

Recent studies show that LLMs can generate personalized learning plans, career advice, and interview preparation content with high accuracy and adaptability.
